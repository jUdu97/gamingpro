Questions

How do I regain progress after disconnect?

If the network disconnects or an in-game error occurs, progress is stored from the last save point.

Can I switch characters mid-tournament?

Character switching is allowed, but limited to 3 chagnes per tournament. Unlimited character switching per game session.

Why is there lag?

Should ghosting or glitching of any kind occur, please contact our support team and they will alert the development team of such inquiries. Use {{contactEmail}} to reach the support team.

When should updates be expected?

Monthly updates at the latest. New versions of the game occur every two weeks. For further questions, contact the support team at {{contactEmail}}.
